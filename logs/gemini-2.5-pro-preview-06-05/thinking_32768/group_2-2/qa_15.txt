### Step-by-Step Reasoning

**Step 1: Define the Speed of Light**

*   **Value:** The speed of light (c) is approximately **299,792,458 meters per second** (m/s), or about 186,282 miles per second.

**Step 2: Define the Speed of Sound**

*   Sound is a mechanical wave, which means it is a vibration that travels through a medium (like air, water, or a solid). It cannot travel through a vacuum because there are no particles to vibrate.
*   The speed of sound is not constant; it changes depending on the medium it is traveling through, as well as the temperature and pressure of that medium.
*   **Value:** In dry air at sea level at 20°C (68°F), the speed of sound is approximately **343 meters per second** (m/s), or about 767 miles per hour.
