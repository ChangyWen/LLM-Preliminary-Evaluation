**Step 4: Clarify a common point of confusion.**

It's important not to confuse the *Roman occupation of Britain* with the *British Empire*. The Romans conquered and ruled the island of Britain (which they called *Britannia*) from 43 AD to around 410 AD. During this time, there was no "British Empire." The land was a province of the Roman Empire, inhabited by Celtic tribes. The political entity we know as the British Empire was formed by the inhabitants of that island more than a millennium later.
